HOW OFTEN DO YOU RECOMMEND CLEANING FOR A TYPICAL OFFICE ENVIRONMENT?

When it comes to maintaining a clean and professional office environment in Leavenworth, KS, regular cleaning is key. Here's a breakdown of how often we recommend cleaning for a typical office:

1. Daily Cleaning:

  • Emptying trash cans and replacing liners to keep the office looking tidy and fresh.
  • Sweeping and mopping floors to remove dirt and grime that can accumulate throughout the day.

2. Weekly Cleaning:

  • Dusting surfaces such as desks, shelves, and electronics to prevent the buildup of dust and allergens.
  • Disinfecting high-touch areas like doorknobs, light switches, and shared equipment for a healthy work environment.

3. Monthly Cleaning:

  • Deep cleaning carpets and upholstery to remove embedded dirt and stains, creating a fresh and inviting workspace.
  • Polishing floors to maintain a professional appearance and extend the life of your flooring.
