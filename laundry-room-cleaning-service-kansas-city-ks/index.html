WHAT DO I DO WITH MY PETS WHEN YOU ARE CLEANING?

When you schedule a cleaning service for your laundry room in Kansas City, KS, it's natural to wonder what to do with your pets during the process. Here are a few tips to ensure a smooth cleaning experience:

  • During the cleaning service, it's best to keep your pets in a separate room or area where they feel comfortable and secure.
  • If possible, it's a good idea to inform the cleaning team about any pets in the house to ensure they are aware and can work around them safely.
