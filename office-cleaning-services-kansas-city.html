Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you have questions about commercial cleaning services. Here are the answers Kansas City business owners ask us most frequently.

Can you clean during business hours?

While we prefer after-hours cleaning to avoid disrupting your operations, we can certainly accommodate daytime cleaning if that works better for your business. We're very experienced at working quietly and efficiently around employees and clients. We'll discuss your preferences during our initial consultation and create a schedule that works perfectly for your specific needs and workflow.

Do you provide cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes, we come fully equipped with all professional-grade supplies and commercial cleaning equipment needed for your office. This includes everything from industrial vacuums and floor care equipment to commercial disinfectants and eco-friendly products. You'll never need to purchase or store cleaning supplies - we handle everything so you can focus on your business.

How do you ensure security in our office?

Security is our top priority. All our team members undergo thorough background checks and are bonded and insured. We can work with your existing security protocols, including alarm systems, key cards, or building access procedures. We also provide detailed cleaning checklists and can work around sensitive areas or equipment as needed.

What's included in your office cleaning service?

Our standard office cleaning includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, cleaning and sanitizing restrooms, emptying trash and recycling, cleaning break room areas, wiping down desks and common surfaces, and sanitizing high-touch areas like door handles and light switches. We can customize our service to include additional tasks specific to your business needs.

How often should our office be professionally cleaned?

The frequency depends on your office size, number of employees, and type of business. Most offices benefit from daily or weekly cleaning, while some smaller offices do well with bi-weekly service. High-traffic areas and businesses that see many clients typically need more frequent cleaning. We'll assess your specific situation and recommend the optimal schedule during our consultation.

What if we're not satisfied with the cleaning?

We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our service, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to address your concerns at no charge. We also conduct regular quality checks and welcome feedback to ensure we're consistently meeting your expectations and business standards.
