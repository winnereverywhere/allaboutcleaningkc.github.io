Frequently Asked Questions About Our House Cleaning Services

Get answers to the most common questions about our professional house cleaning services in Kansas City. We're here to make your home spotless and your life easier.

What's included in your house cleaning service?

Our comprehensive house cleaning service covers all the essentials to make your home shine. We clean and sanitize bathrooms, including toilets, showers, tubs, mirrors, and floors. In the kitchen, we tackle countertops, appliances, sinks, and cabinet fronts. We dust all surfaces, vacuum carpets, mop hard floors, and clean light fixtures. We also empty trash bins and make beds. Every cleaning is customized to your specific needs and preferences, ensuring you get exactly what you want for your home.

How often should I schedule house cleaning?

The frequency of house cleaning depends on your lifestyle, family size, and personal preferences. Most families benefit from weekly or bi-weekly cleaning to maintain a consistently clean and healthy environment. Busy professionals often choose weekly service, while families with older children might prefer bi-weekly. Monthly cleaning works well for those who maintain their homes regularly but want professional deep cleaning. We’ll work with you to determine the perfect schedule that fits your needs and budget, and you can always adjust as your situation changes.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

You don’t need to be home during our cleaning service, which gives you the freedom to go about your day while we make your house spotless. Many of our clients provide us with access instructions and go to work, run errands, or enjoy leisure time. Our team is fully insured, bonded, and background-checked for your peace of mind. If you prefer to be present, that’s perfectly fine too. We’re flexible and work around your schedule to provide the convenience you deserve.

What should I do to prepare for house cleaning?

Minimal preparation is needed, which is one of the benefits of our professional service. We recommend picking up personal items, toys, and clutter so we can focus on deep cleaning rather than organizing. Secure any valuables or important documents you prefer we don’t handle. If you have pets, let us know their temperament and any special instructions. Clear kitchen counters and bathroom surfaces of personal toiletries when possible. Our team brings all necessary equipment and supplies, so you don’t need to worry about providing anything.

Are your cleaning products safe for my family and pets?

Absolutely! We prioritize the health and safety of your family and pets by using high-quality, effective cleaning products that are safe when used properly. Our standard products are thoroughly tested and EPA-approved. We also offer eco-friendly and green cleaning options for clients with allergies, sensitivities, or environmental concerns. If you have specific product preferences or restrictions, just let us know and we’ll accommodate your needs. Your family’s safety is our top priority, and we’re happy to discuss any concerns you might have.

What if I'm not completely satisfied with the cleaning?

Your complete satisfaction is our guarantee. If you’re not happy with any aspect of our cleaning service, simply contact us within 24 hours and we’ll return to address the issue at no additional charge. We stand behind our work and want every client to be thrilled with the results. Our goal is to exceed your expectations every time. We also encourage feedback so we can continuously improve our service and ensure we’re meeting your specific needs and preferences.
