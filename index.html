Frequently Asked Questions

We know you have questions about our house cleaning services. Here are the answers to what Kansas City homeowners ask us most.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all! Many of our clients prefer to be out while we work, giving them the wonderful surprise of returning to a spotless home. We're fully bonded and insured, and our trusted team members have been thoroughly background-checked. You can provide us with access instructions, and we'll secure your home when we're finished. If you prefer to be home, that's perfectly fine too - whatever makes you most comfortable.

What cleaning products do you use?

We use high-quality, professional-grade cleaning products that are effective yet safe for your family and pets. Our standard products are eco-friendly and non-toxic whenever possible. If you have specific product preferences or allergies, just let us know - we're happy to accommodate special requests and can even use your preferred products if you provide them.

How long does a typical house cleaning take?

The time varies depending on your home's size and the type of cleaning needed. A standard weekly cleaning for an average 3-bedroom home typically takes 2-3 hours, while deep cleaning can take 4-6 hours. We always provide an estimated timeframe during your consultation so you know exactly what to expect.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?

Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with any aspect of our service, simply call us within 24 hours, and we'll return to address your concerns at no additional charge. We're not satisfied until you're thrilled with the results.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes! We arrive fully equipped with all the professional cleaning supplies, tools, and equipment needed to make your home sparkle. From HEPA-filtered vacuums to microfiber cloths and eco-friendly products, we bring everything necessary. This means you never have to worry about running out of supplies or buying expensive cleaning equipment.

Can I customize what gets cleaned?

Absolutely! We understand that every home and every homeowner is different. During your initial consultation, we'll discuss your priorities and preferences to create a customized cleaning plan that focuses on what matters most to you. Whether you want extra attention on certain rooms or have areas you'd prefer we skip, we'll tailor our service to your specific needs.
